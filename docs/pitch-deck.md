# ANERTiC — Pitch Deck Summary

## Problem

Homeowners and businesses with solar, batteries, and EVs have **no unified view** of their energy. They overpay for grid power, underuse solar, and charge EVs at the worst times — because they can't see the full picture.

## Solution

**ANERTiC** combines proprietary hardware — smart meters and EV chargers — with an AI-powered platform to give you complete energy control. Also integrates with third-party devices from any vendor.

## How It Works

1. **Connect** — Plug in devices via MQTT, REST API, or OCPP (EV chargers)
2. **Monitor** — See live energy flow: Solar + Grid → Battery → Home + EV
3. **Optimize** — AI analyzes patterns and recommends when to charge, store, or export

## Key Features

- **Live Energy Flow** — Real-time visualization of every watt, source to consumption
- **AI Insights** — Daily personalized recommendations to reduce costs and maximize self-sufficiency
- **EV Fleet Management** — OCPP 1.6/2.0.1 native, remote start/stop, per-connector analytics
- **Room-Level Monitoring** — Know which rooms consume the most, down to individual devices
- **Multi-Site** — Manage multiple properties from one account

## Target Users

- **Homeowners** with solar + battery + EV setups
- **Property managers** with multi-site portfolios
- **Small businesses** optimizing energy costs
- **EV fleet operators** managing charging infrastructure
